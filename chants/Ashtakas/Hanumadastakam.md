## 🌸 Opening Doha

<div class="verse-card">

### 🪔 Doha 1

**श्रीगुरु चरन सरोज रज, निज मन मुकुर सुधारि।**  
*Shri Guru Charan Saroj Raj, Nij Man Mukur Sudhari*  
💫 I purify my mind with the dust of my Guru’s lotus feet.

**बरनऊं रघुबर बिमल जसु, जो दायक फल चारि॥**  
*Baranau Raghuvar Bimal Jasu, Jo Dayaku Phal Chari*  
💫 I sing Lord Rama’s pure glory which grants life’s four blessings.

</div>

# 🌿 Hanuman Chalisa Verses

<div class="verse-card">

### 1️⃣ Chaupai 1

**जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुन सागर।**  
*Jai Hanuman Gyan Gun Sagar*  
✨ Victory to Hanuman, ocean of wisdom and virtues.

**जय कपीस तिहुँ लोक उजागर॥**  
*Jai Kapis Tihun Lok Ujagar*  
✨ You illuminate all three worlds.

</div>

---

<div class="verse-card">

### 2️⃣ Chaupai 2

**राम दूत अतुलित बल धामा।**  
*Ram Doot Atulit Bal Dhama*  
✨ Messenger of Rama and abode of immense strength.

**अंजनि पुत्र पवनसुत नामा॥**  
*Anjani Putra Pavan Sut Nama*  
✨ Son of Anjani and the wind god.

</div>

## 🌸 Closing Prayer

<div class="verse-card">

**पवन तनय संकट हरन, मंगल मूरति रूप।**  
*Pavan Tanay Sankat Haran, Mangal Murti Roop*  
✨ O son of wind, remover of troubles.

**राम लखन सीता सहित, हृदय बसहु सुर भूप॥**  
*Ram Lakhan Sita Sahit, Hriday Basahu Sur Bhoop*  
✨ Reside in my heart along with Rama, Lakshmana, and Sita.

</div>
