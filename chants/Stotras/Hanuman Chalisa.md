## 🌸 Doha (Opening)

**श्रीगुरु चरन सरोज रज, निज मन मुकुर सुधारि।**
*Shri Guru Charan Saroj Raj, Nij Man Mukur Sudhari*
**Meaning:** I purify my mind with the dust of my Guru’s lotus feet.

**बरनऊं रघुबर बिमल जसु, जो दायक फल चारि॥**
*Baranau Raghuvar Bimal Jasu, Jo Dayaku Phal Chari*
**Meaning:** I describe the pure glory of Lord Rama, which grants the four goals of life.

# 🌿 Chaupais

---

### 1

**जय हनुमान ज्ञान गुन सागर।**
*Jai Hanuman Gyan Gun Sagar*
**Meaning:** Victory to Hanuman, ocean of wisdom and virtues.

**जय कपीस तिहुँ लोक उजागर॥**
*Jai Kapis Tihun Lok Ujagar*
**Meaning:** You illuminate all three worlds.

---

### 2

**राम दूत अतुलित बल धामा।**
*Ram Doot Atulit Bal Dhama*
**Meaning:** Messenger of Rama and abode of immense strength.

**अंजनि पुत्र पवनसुत नामा॥**
*Anjani Putra Pavan Sut Nama*
**Meaning:** Son of Anjani and the wind god.

---

### 3

**महाबीर बिक्रम बजरंगी।**
*Mahabir Bikram Bajrangi*
**Meaning:** Mighty and powerful like a thunderbolt.

**कुमति निवार सुमति के संगी॥**
*Kumati Nivar Sumati Ke Sangi*
**Meaning:** You remove bad thoughts and grant wisdom.

---

### 4

**कंचन बरन बिराज सुबेसा।**
*Kanchan Baran Biraj Subesa*
**Meaning:** Golden-hued and beautifully adorned.

**कानन कुंडल कुंचित केसा॥**
*Kanan Kundal Kunchit Kesa*
**Meaning:** Wearing earrings with curly hair.

---

### 5

**हाथ वज्र औ ध्वजा बिराजे।**
*Hath Vajra Aur Dhwaja Biraje*
**Meaning:** Holding a thunderbolt and a flag.

**कांधे मूंज जनेऊ साजे॥**
*Kandhe Moonj Janeu Saaje*
**Meaning:** Sacred thread adorns your shoulder.

---

### 6

**शंकर सुवन केसरी नंदन।**
*Shankar Suvan Kesari Nandan*
**Meaning:** Son of Shiva and Kesari.

**तेज प्रताप महा जग वंदन॥**
*Tej Pratap Maha Jag Vandan*
**Meaning:** Your glory is revered across the world.

---

### 7

**विद्यावान गुनी अति चातुर।**
*Vidyavan Guni Ati Chatur*
**Meaning:** Wise, virtuous, and intelligent.

**राम काज करिबे को आतुर॥**
*Ram Kaj Karibe Ko Atur*
**Meaning:** Always eager to serve Lord Rama.

---

### 8

**प्रभु चरित्र सुनिबे को रसिया।**
*Prabhu Charitra Sunibe Ko Rasiya*
**Meaning:** Lover of listening to Rama’s stories.

**राम लखन सीता मन बसिया॥**
*Ram Lakhan Sita Man Basiya*
**Meaning:** You dwell in their hearts.

---

### 9

**सूक्ष्म रूप धरि सियहिं दिखावा।**
*Sukshma Roop Dhari Siyahi Dikhava*
**Meaning:** Took a small form to appear before Sita.

**बिकट रूप धरि लंक जरावा॥**
*Vikat Roop Dhari Lanka Jarava*
**Meaning:** Took a fierce form to burn Lanka.

---

### 10

**भीम रूप धरि असुर संहारे।**
*Bheem Roop Dhari Asur Sanhare*
**Meaning:** Destroyed demons in a mighty form.

**रामचंद्र के काज सवारे॥**
*Ramchandra Ke Kaj Savare*
**Meaning:** Completed Lord Rama’s mission.

---

### 11

**लाय सजीवन लखन जियाए।**
*Laye Sanjeevan Lakhan Jiyaye*
**Meaning:** Brought Sanjeevani to revive Lakshmana.

**श्रीरघुबीर हरषि उर लाए॥**
*Shri Raghubir Harashi Ur Laye*
**Meaning:** Rama embraced you with joy.

---

### 12

**रघुपति कीन्ही बहुत बड़ाई।**
*Raghupati Kinhi Bahut Badai*
**Meaning:** Rama greatly praised you.

**तुम मम प्रिय भरतहि सम भाई॥**
*Tum Mam Priya Bharat Hi Sam Bhai*
**Meaning:** You are as dear as Bharata.

---

### 13

**सहस बदन तुम्हरो जस गावैं।**
*Sahas Badan Tumharo Jas Gaave*
**Meaning:** Thousand mouths sing your praise.

**अस कहि श्रीपति कंठ लगावैं॥**
*As Kahi Shripati Kanth Lagave*
**Meaning:** Lord embraced you lovingly.

---

### 14

**सनकादिक ब्रह्मादि मुनीसा।**
*Sanakadik Brahmadi Munisa*
**Meaning:** Great sages like Sanaka and Brahma praise you.

**नारद सारद सहित अहीसा॥**
*Narad Sarad Sahit Ahisa*
**Meaning:** Narada, Saraswati, and Sheshnag sing your glory.

---

### 15

**जम कुबेर दिगपाल जहाँ ते।**
*Yam Kuber Digpal Jahan Te*
**Meaning:** Yama, Kubera, and guardians of directions honor you.

**कवि कोविद कहि सके कहाँ ते॥**
*Kavi Kovid Kahi Sake Kahan Te*
**Meaning:** Even great poets cannot describe your greatness fully.

---

### 16

**तुम उपकार सुग्रीवहिं कीन्हा।**
*Tum Upkar Sugrivahin Kinha*
**Meaning:** You helped Sugriva in his difficult time.

**राम मिलाय राज पद दीन्हा॥**
*Ram Milay Raj Pad Dinha*
**Meaning:** You united him with Rama and restored his kingdom.

---

### 17

**तुम्हरो मंत्र विभीषण माना।**
*Tumharo Mantra Vibhishan Mana*
**Meaning:** Vibhishana accepted your wise counsel.

**लंकेश्वर भए सब जग जाना॥**
*Lankeshwar Bhaye Sab Jag Jana*
**Meaning:** Thus he became king of Lanka.

---

### 18

**युग सहस्र योजन पर भानू।**
*Yug Sahasra Yojan Par Bhanu*
**Meaning:** The sun was thousands of miles away.

**लील्यो ताहि मधुर फल जानू॥**
*Leelyo Tahi Madhur Phal Janu*
**Meaning:** Yet you swallowed it thinking it was a sweet fruit.

---

### 19

**प्रभु मुद्रिका मेलि मुख माहीं।**
*Prabhu Mudrika Meli Mukh Mahi*
**Meaning:** Holding Rama’s ring in your mouth,

**जलधि लांघि गए अचरज नाहीं॥**
*Jaladhi Langhi Gaye Acharaj Nahi*
**Meaning:** You crossed the ocean effortlessly.

---

### 20

**दुर्गम काज जगत के जेते।**
*Durgam Kaj Jagat Ke Jete*
**Meaning:** All difficult tasks in this world,

**सुगम अनुग्रह तुम्हरे तेते॥**
*Sugam Anugrah Tumhare Tete*
**Meaning:** Become easy through your grace.

---

### 21

**राम दुआरे तुम रखवारे।**
*Ram Duware Tum Rakhware*
**Meaning:** You guard the door of Lord Rama.

**होत न आज्ञा बिनु पैसारे॥**
*Hot Na Agya Binu Paisare*
**Meaning:** No one enters without your permission.

---

### 22

**सब सुख लहै तुम्हारी सरना।**
*Sab Sukh Lahai Tumhari Sarna*
**Meaning:** All happiness comes to those in your refuge.

**तुम रक्षक काहू को डरना॥**
*Tum Rakshak Kahu Ko Darna*
**Meaning:** With you as protector, there is no fear.

---

### 23

**आपन तेज सम्हारो आपै।**
*Aapan Tej Samharo Aapai*
**Meaning:** Only you can control your immense power.

**तीनों लोक हांक ते कांपै॥**
*Teenon Lok Hank Te Kanpai*
**Meaning:** Your roar shakes all three worlds.

---

### 24

**भूत पिशाच निकट नहिं आवै।**
*Bhoot Pishach Nikat Nahi Aavai*
**Meaning:** Evil spirits cannot come near,

**महाबीर जब नाम सुनावै॥**
*Mahabir Jab Naam Sunavai*
**Meaning:** When your mighty name is chanted.

---

### 25

**नासै रोग हरै सब पीरा।**
*Nasai Rog Harai Sab Peera*
**Meaning:** You remove disease and suffering.

**जपत निरंतर हनुमत बीरा॥**
*Japat Nirantar Hanumat Beera*
**Meaning:** Constant chanting of your name heals pain.

---

### 26

**संकट ते हनुमान छुड़ावै।**
*Sankat Te Hanuman Chhudavai*
**Meaning:** Hanuman frees devotees from troubles.

**मन क्रम वचन ध्यान जो लावै॥**
*Man Kram Vachan Dhyan Jo Lavai*
**Meaning:** Those who meditate with mind, action, and speech.

---

### 27

**सब पर राम तपस्वी राजा।**
*Sab Par Ram Tapasvi Raja*
**Meaning:** Rama is supreme king over all.

**तिन के काज सकल तुम साजा॥**
*Tin Ke Kaj Sakal Tum Saja*
**Meaning:** You accomplish all His divine work.

---

### 28

**और मनोरथ जो कोई लावै।**
*Aur Manorath Jo Koi Lavai*
**Meaning:** Whoever brings wishes before you,

**सोई अमित जीवन फल पावै॥**
*Soi Amit Jeevan Phal Pavai*
**Meaning:** Receives abundant blessings.

---

### 29

**चारों जुग परताप तुम्हारा।**
*Charon Yug Partap Tumhara*
**Meaning:** Your glory shines through all four ages.

**है परसिद्ध जगत उजियारा॥**
*Hai Parsiddh Jagat Ujiyara*
**Meaning:** Your fame illuminates the world.

---

### 30

**साधु संत के तुम रखवारे।**
*Sadhu Sant Ke Tum Rakhware*
**Meaning:** You protect saints and sages.

**असुर निकंदन राम दुलारे॥**
*Asur Nikandan Ram Dulare*
**Meaning:** Destroyer of demons, beloved of Rama.

---

### 31

**अष्ट सिद्धि नौ निधि के दाता।**
*Ashta Siddhi Nau Nidhi Ke Data*
**Meaning:** Bestower of eight powers and nine treasures.

**अस बर दीन जानकी माता॥**
*As Bar Din Janki Mata*
**Meaning:** This boon was granted by Mother Sita.

---

### 32

**राम रसायन तुम्हरे पासा।**
*Ram Rasayan Tumhare Pasa*
**Meaning:** You possess the nectar of Rama’s devotion.

**सदा रहो रघुपति के दासा॥**
*Sada Raho Raghupati Ke Dasa*
**Meaning:** Forever remain servant of Rama.

---

### 33

**तुम्हरे भजन राम को पावै।**
*Tumhare Bhajan Ram Ko Pavai*
**Meaning:** Through devotion to you, one reaches Rama.

**जनम जनम के दुख बिसरावै॥**
*Janam Janam Ke Dukh Bisaravai*
**Meaning:** Sorrows of many lifetimes are removed.

---

### 34

**अंतकाल रघुबर पुर जाई।**
*Antakal Raghubar Pur Jai*
**Meaning:** At life’s end one reaches Rama’s abode.

**जहाँ जन्म हरि भक्त कहाई॥**
*Jahan Janma Hari Bhakt Kahai*
**Meaning:** And is reborn as devotee of God.

---

### 35

**और देवता चित्त न धरई।**
*Aur Devta Chitt Na Dharai*
**Meaning:** No need to worship other deities.

**हनुमत सेइ सर्व सुख करई॥**
*Hanumat Sei Sarva Sukh Karai*
**Meaning:** Serving Hanuman brings all happiness.

---

### 36

**संकट कटै मिटै सब पीरा।**
*Sankat Katai Mitai Sab Peera*
**Meaning:** Troubles vanish and pain ends.

**जो सुमिरै हनुमत बलबीरा॥**
*Jo Sumirai Hanumat Balbeera*
**Meaning:** For those who remember mighty Hanuman.

---

### 37

**जय जय जय हनुमान गोसाईं।**
*Jai Jai Jai Hanuman Gosai*
**Meaning:** Victory, victory to Lord Hanuman!

**कृपा करहु गुरु देव की नाईं॥**
*Kripa Karahu Gurudev Ki Nai*
**Meaning:** Bless us like a compassionate Guru.

---

### 38

**जो सत बार पाठ कर कोई।**
*Jo Sat Bar Path Kar Koi*
**Meaning:** Whoever recites this one hundred times,

**छूटहि बंदि महा सुख होई॥**
*Chhutahi Bandi Maha Sukh Hoi*
**Meaning:** Is freed from bondage and gains joy.

---

### 39

**जो यह पढ़े हनुमान चालीसा।**
*Jo Yah Padhe Hanuman Chalisa*
**Meaning:** Whoever reads Hanuman Chalisa,

**होय सिद्धि साखी गौरीसा॥**
*Hoye Siddhi Sakhi Gaurisa*
**Meaning:** Gains success, Lord Shiva is witness.

---

### 40

**तुलसीदास सदा हरि चेरा।**
*Tulsidas Sada Hari Chera*
**Meaning:** Tulsidas is forever servant of Hari.

**कीजै नाथ हृदय महँ डेरा॥**
*Kijai Nath Hriday Mah Dera*
**Meaning:** O Lord Hanuman, dwell in my heart always.

---


## 🌸 Closing Doha

**पवन तनय संकट हरन, मंगल मूरति रूप।**
*Pavan Tanay Sankat Haran, Mangal Murti Roop*
**Meaning:** O son of wind, remover of troubles, embodiment of auspiciousness.

**राम लखन सीता सहित, हृदय बसहु सुर भूप॥**
*Ram Lakhan Sita Sahit, Hriday Basahu Sur Bhoop*
**Meaning:** Reside in my heart along with Rama, Lakshmana, and Sita.
